Services

Across the years we have offered multiple services for venues such as informal safety consultations, Ask for Angela training, venue layout consultations, event consultations. We are available to DJ events as Make Noise DJ Collective, and we are able to provide DJ workshops. We are always keen to work with people on local events and gigs. Please contact us at makenoisehull@gmail.com for more information on how you can access these services or bring your ideas to us.

Resources

  • Safety Measures in Hull Nightlife Scene: In this document we compiled the safety measures put into place by Hull nightclubs and bars in response to the rise of spiking incidents nationally.
  • Good Night Out: Good Night Out campaign offer training and GNO certification for venues, musical festivals, student unions and more looking to create a safe space - for fun, not fear.
  • Attitude is Everything: Attitude is Everything are an organisation focused on improving accessibility for disabled and HoH individuals in the live music scene. Offering training, consultancy, accreditation for venues and events.
  • SAFERSOUNDS PARTNERSHIPS Ask for Angela and WAVE: Safersounds are the facilitators of the very popular 'Ask For Angela' programme and of the WAVE (Welfare and Vulnerability Engagement) programme. They offer training for those in licensed premises to spot and cope with vulnerable people in dangerous situations. They also offer Ask for Angela training for venues.
